Technical Debt - A Manager's Guide

Inscreva-se agora!

Technical Debt is a catchall term that we use for entropy in software development. As systems grow they can become difficult to change. When this happens we have fewer options as we add new features, and the options we do have become costly. Fortunately, we can identify the vital parts of our systems and proactively manage technical debt. This one­day course presents an actionable definition of technical debt, an analysis of its root causes, and a series of preventative and corrective actions that teams and managers can take to keep systems nimble as they grow. Topics include: ­The Effects of Software Change ­A Pragmatic Definition of Technical Debt ­Design Quality Trend Analysis ­Design Decision Cards ­Feature Probes ­Change Progression Planning The format of the course is lecture, simulations, team exercises and discussion.
