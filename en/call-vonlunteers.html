Call for Volunteers

If you are an active person who enjoys to work in a team, Agile Brazil 2015 volunteers’ program is a great opportunity for you to assist in the conference, putting Agile in practice and innovating in several forms!

We are looking for excited people with strong sense of commitment so we can put together a diverse team, who are willing to share knowledge and continuously evolve, during the conference.

Please read the information below about the volunteer program in the FAQ prepared by the volunteers and veterans of previous editions.

FAQ

Who are we looking for?

If you are engaged in the agile community (or want to be!), if you want to participate in a very important conference, and also wants to make Agile Brazil happen, you are the person we’re looking for as a volunteer at Agile Brazil 2015.

What do the volunteers do?

The main job of a volunteer is to make sure that the event runs nicely. For this, you will help receiving the participants, watching the rooms, taking feedbacks after the sessions, supporting the speakers and the event in general and also participating in a discussion list with other people as interested as you.

How many hours / days the volunteers need to be available?

On the previous day of the event beginning, the volunteers will be introduced and will participate of a training session and the kit’s set up.

During the event, we expect that the volunteer has the maximum a day and a half full of activities (12 hours). That is, three working turns (morning or afternoon) of 4 hours. The working turn is chosen by the volunteer.

Also, we have some stand-up meetings before or after the event time. The volunteer team also uses Agile methodologies!

And what do I get?

Volunteering is also a way to give a chance to those who could not come in other way: using your effort and work, you will have access to the 3 days of the event, and will have richer opportunities of interaction with speakers and participants.

How to apply?

You shoud complete this form and wait our contact. Enjoy!

When do I know if I am in?

The volunteer Program dates are:

  • June, 03th: application form is available;
  • July, 03th: application deadline (timezone warning: BRT);
  • July, 10th: selection results are out;
  • July, 18th: confirmation deadline.
